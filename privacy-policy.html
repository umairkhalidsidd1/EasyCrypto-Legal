Privacy Policy

StockAI

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

This privacy notice describes how StockAI operates with respect to your privacy when you use the app and its services. StockAI is designed with privacy in mind - we prioritize keeping your data secure and under your control. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at umairkhalidsidd@gmail.com.

1 What Information Do We Collect?

Local Data Storage

StockAI stores data locally on your device to provide its core functionality. This includes:

  • Learning Progress: Your lesson completion status, quiz scores, and achievements
  • Chart Analysis History: Your saved chart analyses and preferences
  • App Settings: Your personalized settings and preferences
  • Practice Data: Your practice session history and performance metrics

✅ Your data stays on your device. StockAI does not require account creation or personal information to use the app's core features.

Automatically Collected Information

StockAI may collect anonymous technical information to improve app performance:

  • Device Information: Anonymous device type and operating system version (no unique identifiers)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous aggregated usage statistics to understand app performance
  • Error Reports: Anonymous crash logs and diagnostic data to improve app stability

All collected data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2 How Do We Use Your Information?

Since StockAI primarily stores data locally, your information is used to:

  • Track your learning progress and display achievements
  • Save your chart analysis history for future reference
  • Personalize your app experience based on your preferences
  • Improve app stability through anonymous crash reporting

You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time from your device settings or within the app.

3 Third-Party Services

StockAI may integrate with third-party services to provide certain features:

  • Cryptocurrency Data: We fetch real-time and historical crypto market data from third-party APIs
  • AI Analysis: Chart analysis features may use AI services to provide insights

When using these features, only the necessary data (such as chart images or market queries) is transmitted. No personal information is shared with these services.

4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

No, StockAI does not use cookies or tracking technologies. All app functionality relies on local storage within your device. We do not track your activity across websites or apps.

5 How Long Do We Keep Your Data?

StockAI does not store your data on our servers. All your data is stored locally on your device and remains there until you choose to delete it.

You can delete your local data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual analyses or progress from within the app
  • Clearing the app's data from your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app (which removes all local data)

6 Children's Privacy

StockAI is not intended for children under 13 years old. The app contains educational content about cryptocurrency and financial markets, which is designed for adult users.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, it will be deleted immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child's data has been collected should contact us at umairkhalidsidd@gmail.com.

7 Data Sharing

🔒 We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers, marketers, or external companies.

Your chart analyses, learning progress, and app preferences never leave your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties without your explicit action.

8 Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app at all times
  • Deletion: Delete any or all data at any time through the app or device settings
  • Portability: Your data can be backed up using your device's native backup features
  • Control: Full control over what data is stored and when it is deleted

9 Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All local data is stored using secure storage mechanisms provided by your device's operating system. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10 Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date. If significant changes occur, users will be notified through the app.

We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed about how their data is handled.

Questions or Concerns?

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

umairkhalidsidd@gmail.com
