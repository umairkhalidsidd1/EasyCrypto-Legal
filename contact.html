? Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your learning progress in the app settings. Go to Settings > Data Management > Reset Progress. This will clear all your quiz scores and lesson completions.

The app is not loading market data

Please check your internet connection and try refreshing the app. If the issue persists, clear the app cache or reinstall the app. Contact us if the problem continues.

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it by going to your device settings > Apps > EasyCrypto > Storage > Clear Data. This will remove all local data from your device.

I found a bug in the app

We appreciate bug reports! Please email us with details about the issue, including your device type, iOS/Android version, and steps to reproduce the problem.
